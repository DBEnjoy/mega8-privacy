Delete Your Data - Mega 8

If you would like to request the deletion of your data associated with the Mega 8 app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to dbenjoygames@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request - Mega 8
  3. Include any relevant details to help us identify your data (e.g., your device type or Google Play username)

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm the deletion via email.

What data will be deleted:
  • Locally stored game data (scores, settings, preferences)
  • Any data associated with Google Play Games (leaderboard scores, achievements)
What data is managed by third parties:
  • AdMob: Advertising data is managed by Google. You can manage your ad preferences at Google Ad Settings.
  • Google Play Services: Data is managed through your Google Account settings.

For more information, please read our Privacy Policy.
